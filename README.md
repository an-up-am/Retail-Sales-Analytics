## Overview

This project presents an interactive Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel to analyze sales performance across products, regions, customer demographics, and sales channels.

The dashboard processes 9,648 retail transactions and provides actionable insights into revenue trends, product performance, geographic distribution, and customer purchasing behavior through dynamic visualizations and KPI reporting.

---

## Business Problem

Retail organizations generate large volumes of transactional data, making it difficult to identify key revenue drivers and growth opportunities.

This dashboard was developed to answer the following business questions:

* Which products contribute the most revenue?
* Which states and regions drive business performance?
* How do customer demographics influence sales?
* Which sales channel performs best?
* What seasonal trends exist across the year?

## Key Insights

### Revenue Analysis

* Generated $120.17M in total revenue across 9,648 transactions.
* Average revenue per transaction reached $12.46K.

### Geographic Insights

* Top 5 states contributed 29.4% of total revenue.
* New York generated the highest revenue ($8.67M).
* West region accounted for 30.3% of overall sales.

### Product Insights

* Men's Street Footwear generated $27.68M in revenue.
* Footwear categories represented the largest share of total sales.

### Sales Channel Insights

* Online sales contributed 37.4% of total revenue.
* Online channel outperformed In-store sales by approximately $9.3M.

### Customer Insights

* Male customers contributed 53.9% of total revenue.
* Footwear products were the dominant purchase category across customer segments.

### Seasonality Insights

* July recorded the highest monthly revenue.
* Revenue remained consistently strong during Q3, indicating seasonal demand patterns.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Increase inventory allocation in top-performing states.
* Expand digital marketing investments for the Online channel.
* Focus future product promotions on footwear categories.
* Launch targeted campaigns during peak sales months.
* Improve weekend promotional strategies to increase Saturday sales performance.
